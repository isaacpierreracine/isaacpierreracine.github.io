### SHORT BIOGRAPHY.  

I studied fine arts in Quebec and built a career as an art director and filmmaker in cinema and advertising before embarking on a transition toward the visual arts through experimentation in fabrication, design, and object-making. This trajectory led me to explore collaborative creative processes and to develop a mastery of new technologies.
Over the past few years I have developed a singular artistic practice in sculpture and kinetic installation based on the circularity of materials and energy autonomy.

I grew up in a small village in Quebec, and have lived and worked in Spain for more than 30 years, in New York and in Haiti. I am a self-taught person, curious and passionate about new learning. I have a professional conscience and extensive experience in the creative process. My view of the world is broad and open, and I never tire of experimenting with life.
